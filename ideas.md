# Project Ideas & Future Improvements

A running list of ideas for future enhancements to our projects.

---

## Freshbuild SEO Improvements (Future)

### 1. Google Business Profile Optimization
- Claim/optimize Google Business Profile for "Freshbuild" in Rochester, NY
- Add photos of work, services offered
- Encourage clients to leave reviews
- Post regular updates about projects
- **Impact:** Shows up in Google Maps and local search results

### 2. Client Reviews & Testimonials
- Ask satisfied clients for reviews on Google Business Profile
- Display testimonials on website with names and businesses
- Link to Google reviews from website
- **Impact:** Builds trust and improves local SEO rankings

### 3. Location-Specific Landing Pages
- Create pages targeting nearby cities: "Web Design in [City], NY"
- Examples: Brighton, Pittsford, Victor, Henrietta, Penfield, Webster
- Each page with local content about serving that area
- **Impact:** Rank for searches in surrounding areas

### 4. Blog/Content Marketing
- Write helpful articles: "How Much Should a Website Cost?", "5 Signs You Need a Website Redesign", etc.
- Target long-tail keywords that potential clients search
- Establish expertise and attract organic traffic
- **Impact:** Long-term traffic growth and authority building

### 5. Technical SEO Improvements
- Optimize page speed (compress images, minify CSS/JS)
- Improve Core Web Vitals scores
- Add internal linking between pages
- Optimize images with descriptive filenames and alt tags
- **Impact:** Better user experience and search rankings

### 6. Video Content
- Create short videos showing before/after of website refreshes
- Tutorial videos for small business owners
- Post on YouTube and embed on website
- **Impact:** Engage visitors and rank in video search results

### 7. Email Marketing for Past Clients
- Build email list of past clients
- Send monthly newsletter with web tips
- Offer maintenance packages or new services
- **Impact:** Repeat business and referrals
