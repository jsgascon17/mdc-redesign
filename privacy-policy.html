Privacy Policy

How MDC Assembly LLC collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: May 31, 2026

MDC Assembly LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through our website at mdisplays.com, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

We only collect the information you choose to provide through our contact form. This may include your:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name
  • Details about your project that you include in your message

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit solely to respond to your inquiry, prepare a quote, and communicate with you about your furniture or fixture installation project. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Form Processing

Our contact form is processed by Web3Forms, a third-party form-handling service that delivers your submission to us by email. Information you enter into the form is transmitted to Web3Forms for that purpose and is subject to their privacy practices. We encourage you to review the Web3Forms Privacy Policy.

Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4, a web-analytics service provided by Google, to understand how visitors find and use our website so we can improve it. Google Analytics sets cookies and collects information such as the pages you visit, your approximate location, and general device and browser details. This information is used in aggregate to measure site usage and is not used to personally identify you. You can review the Google Privacy Policy, and you may opt out of Google Analytics on all websites using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Our website is hosted by SiteGround, our hosting provider, which may set essential cookies needed to deliver the site securely and reliably. Aside from Google Analytics and these essential hosting cookies, we do not use advertising or cross-site tracking scripts.

Data Retention

We retain contact-form submissions for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and maintain a record of our business communications. You may request that we delete your information at any time.

Your Choices

You are not required to provide any personal information to browse our website. If you have submitted information and would like it corrected or deleted, contact us using the details below.
