Who We Are

Professional Installation You Can Trust

MDC Assembly LLC is a furniture and fixture installation company based in Ontario, NY. We specialize in commercial furniture installation for offices, retail stores, schools, and hospitality venues throughout Upstate New York.

Our experienced team handles everything from single-room setups to large-scale corporate projects with the same dedication to quality. We understand that your furniture is an investment, and we treat every installation with the care and attention it deserves.

Whether you need office furniture installed, cubicles configured, or a complete corporate relocation, MDC Assembly has the experience and expertise to get the job done right.
