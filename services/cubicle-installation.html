Cubicle Installation

Expert modular workstation and cubicle system installation, reconfiguration, and tear down across Western and Central New York.

Professional Cubicle Installation Services

MDC Assembly LLC specializes in the installation and reconfiguration of cubicle systems and modular workstations. We work with all major manufacturers and handle projects of any size, from small office additions to complete floor buildouts.

Our experienced installers understand the complexity of modern cubicle systems. We ensure proper alignment, secure connections, and seamless integration with your office's electrical and data infrastructure.

Cubicle Systems We Install

  • Panel-based cubicle systems
  • Modular workstation clusters
  • Open-plan benching systems
  • Private office partitions
  • Height-adjustable workstations
  • Collaborative workspace configurations
  • Call center workstation layouts
  • Hot-desking and flexible workspace setups

Our Cubicle Installation Process

We follow a proven process to ensure your cubicle installation is completed efficiently and correctly:

  • Floor Plan Review: We analyze your layout and specifications before installation
  • Inventory Verification: We confirm all components are present and undamaged
  • Base Frame Installation: We set up the structural framework with precision
  • Panel & Component Assembly: We install panels, work surfaces, and storage units
  • Power & Data Integration: We coordinate with your IT team for connectivity
  • Quality Inspection: We verify every workstation meets specifications

Cubicle Reconfiguration Services

Need to reorganize your existing cubicle layout? We provide professional reconfiguration services to help you adapt your workspace to changing needs. Whether you're adding workstations, creating more collaborative spaces, or optimizing your floor plan, our team can efficiently reconfigure your existing systems.

Manufacturers We Work With

Our installers are experienced with cubicle systems from all major manufacturers. We understand the unique requirements of each system and ensure proper installation according to manufacturer specifications.
