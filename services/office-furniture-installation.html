Office Furniture Installation

Professional setup of desks, chairs, conference tables, and complete office systems across Upstate New York.

Expert Office Furniture Installation Services

MDC Assembly LLC provides comprehensive office furniture installation services for businesses throughout Upstate New York. Whether you're setting up a new office, expanding your workspace, or upgrading your furniture, our experienced team ensures every piece is assembled and positioned correctly.

We work with all major furniture manufacturers and handle everything from individual desk assemblies to complete office buildouts. Our installers are trained professionals who understand the importance of getting your workspace operational quickly and correctly.

What We Install

  • Executive desks and workstations
  • Ergonomic office chairs and seating
  • Conference and meeting room tables
  • Filing cabinets and storage systems
  • Bookcases and shelving units
  • Reception area furniture
  • Sit-stand desks and height-adjustable workstations
  • Modular office furniture systems

Our Installation Process

We follow a systematic approach to ensure your office furniture installation goes smoothly:

  • Site Assessment: We review your space and furniture specifications before installation day
  • Scheduling: We work around your business hours to minimize disruption
  • Professional Assembly: Our trained crews assemble all furniture according to manufacturer specifications
  • Placement & Positioning: We position furniture according to your floor plan
  • Cleanup: We remove all packaging materials and leave your space ready to use
  • Final Walkthrough: We ensure everything meets your satisfaction before we leave
