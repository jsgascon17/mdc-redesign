School Furniture Installation

Professional furniture installation for classrooms, libraries, cafeterias, and educational facilities across Upstate New York.

Educational Furniture Installation Experts

MDC Assembly LLC provides professional furniture installation services for schools, colleges, and educational institutions throughout Upstate New York. We understand the unique requirements of educational environments and work efficiently to minimize disruption to your school's operations.

Whether you're outfitting a new school, renovating classrooms, or updating your library, our experienced team handles projects of all sizes with the same commitment to quality and attention to detail.

Educational Spaces We Serve

  • Classrooms and lecture halls
  • Libraries and media centers
  • Cafeterias and dining facilities
  • Administrative offices
  • Science labs and computer labs
  • Gymnasiums and athletic facilities
  • Common areas and student lounges
  • Faculty offices and workrooms

Types of School Furniture We Install

  • Classroom Furniture: Student desks, chairs, teacher desks, storage units
  • Library Furniture: Shelving systems, study carrels, reading tables, circulation desks
  • Cafeteria Furniture: Tables, seating, serving line fixtures
  • Lab Furniture: Lab benches, storage cabinets, specialized workstations
  • Office Furniture: Administrative desks, filing systems, conference tables

Why Schools Choose MDC Assembly

Educational institutions have specific needs when it comes to furniture installation. We understand the importance of completing work during breaks and off-hours, maintaining security protocols, and ensuring all installations meet safety standards.

  • Flexible scheduling around school calendars
  • Background-checked installation crews
  • Experience with educational furniture systems
  • Compliance with safety and accessibility requirements
  • Efficient project completion to minimize disruption
  • Competitive pricing for educational institutions

Summer Installation Projects

Many schools prefer to complete furniture installations during summer break. We offer priority scheduling for educational clients during summer months and can handle large-scale projects to ensure your facilities are ready when students return.
