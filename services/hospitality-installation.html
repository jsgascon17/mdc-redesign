Hospitality Installation

Professional furniture installation for hotels, restaurants, and hospitality venues across Upstate New York.

Hospitality Furniture Installation Experts

MDC Assembly LLC provides professional furniture installation services for the hospitality industry throughout Upstate New York. We understand the unique demands of hotels, restaurants, and event venues, and we work efficiently to meet your opening deadlines.

From boutique hotels to large conference centers, our experienced team handles hospitality furniture installations of all sizes with attention to detail and a commitment to quality.

Hospitality Spaces We Serve

  • Hotel guest rooms and suites
  • Hotel lobbies and common areas
  • Restaurants and dining rooms
  • Bars and lounges
  • Conference and meeting rooms
  • Banquet facilities
  • Spa and wellness areas
  • Outdoor dining and patio spaces

Types of Hospitality Furniture We Install

  • Guest Room Furniture: Beds, dressers, desks, nightstands, seating
  • Lobby Furniture: Reception desks, seating areas, tables
  • Restaurant Furniture: Tables, chairs, booths, bar stools
  • Conference Furniture: Meeting tables, executive chairs, presentation furniture
  • Outdoor Furniture: Patio sets, lounge chairs, umbrellas

Why Hospitality Clients Choose Us

The hospitality industry has tight deadlines and high standards. We understand that your furniture needs to be installed correctly the first time, on schedule, so you can open on time and start serving guests.

  • Experience with hospitality furniture brands and systems
  • Ability to handle large-scale hotel installations
  • Flexible scheduling to meet opening deadlines
  • Attention to detail and quality workmanship
  • Clean, professional installation crews
  • Coordination with general contractors and project managers

New Property Openings

Opening a new hotel or restaurant? We can handle the complete FF&E (Furniture, Fixtures & Equipment) installation. Our team works from your specifications to ensure every room is set up correctly and ready for guests.

Renovations & Refreshes

Updating your property? We can work around your operations to minimize guest impact. We're experienced with phased installations and can coordinate with your schedule to keep disruption to a minimum.
