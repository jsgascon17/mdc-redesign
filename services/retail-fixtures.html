Retail Fixture Installation

Professional installation of display cases, shelving systems, and store fixtures to get your retail space floor-ready.

Get Your Store Floor-Ready

MDC Assembly LLC provides professional retail fixture installation services for stores throughout Upstate New York. From new store buildouts to remodels and seasonal updates, we handle all types of retail fixture installations with precision and efficiency.

We understand that in retail, time is money. Our experienced crews work quickly to minimize store downtime while ensuring every fixture is installed correctly and securely.

Retail Fixtures We Install

  • Display cases and showcases
  • Gondola shelving systems
  • Wall-mounted shelving and slatwall
  • Checkout counters and POS stations
  • Clothing racks and displays
  • Mannequins and display forms
  • Signage and graphics installations
  • Specialty display fixtures
  • Storage and backroom shelving

Retail Environments We Serve

  • Fashion & Apparel: Boutiques, clothing stores, shoe stores
  • Electronics: Tech stores, phone retailers, computer shops
  • Grocery & Food: Supermarkets, convenience stores, specialty food
  • Pharmacy & Health: Drug stores, health food stores
  • Home & Garden: Furniture stores, hardware stores
  • Specialty Retail: Gift shops, jewelry stores, sporting goods

New Store Buildouts

Opening a new location? We can handle the complete fixture installation for your new store. Our team works from your floor plan and fixture specifications to ensure everything is positioned correctly for your grand opening.

Store Remodels & Updates

Refreshing your store layout? We can help with fixture reconfiguration, new fixture installation, and removal of old fixtures. We work around your business hours to minimize disruption to your operations.

  • Flexible scheduling including nights and weekends
  • Fast, efficient installation to minimize downtime
  • Clean, professional crews
  • Competitive pricing
