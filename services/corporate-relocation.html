Corporate Relocation

Full-service office moving with furniture disassembly, transportation, and reassembly to minimize your downtime.

Seamless Office Relocation Services

Moving your office doesn't have to mean days of lost productivity. MDC Assembly LLC provides comprehensive corporate relocation services designed to get your team back to work as quickly as possible. We handle everything from furniture disassembly to setup at your new location.

Our experienced relocation teams understand that every minute of downtime costs your business money. We work efficiently and methodically to ensure your move goes smoothly, whether you're relocating down the hall or across the city.

Our Relocation Services Include

  • Complete furniture disassembly and labeling
  • Secure packing and protection of components
  • Coordination with moving companies
  • Furniture reassembly at new location
  • Workstation and cubicle reconfiguration
  • Floor plan implementation
  • Asset tagging and inventory management
  • Disposal of old or unwanted furniture

Types of Relocations We Handle

  • Complete Office Moves: Full relocation of all furniture and fixtures
  • Department Relocations: Moving specific teams within a building
  • Floor Shuffles: Reorganizing workspace layouts
  • Expansion Projects: Setting up additional office space
  • Consolidation: Combining multiple locations into one

Our Relocation Process

We follow a structured process to ensure your relocation is completed on time and on budget:

  • Pre-Move Planning: We survey both locations and create a detailed move plan
  • Scheduling: We coordinate timing to minimize business disruption
  • Disassembly: Our team carefully disassembles and labels all furniture
  • Move Coordination: We work with your moving company or provide recommendations
  • Reassembly: We set up everything at your new location according to your floor plan
  • Final Walkthrough: We ensure everything is in place and functional

Minimize Downtime

We offer flexible scheduling including evening and weekend moves to keep your business running. Our goal is to have your team productive in their new space as quickly as possible.
